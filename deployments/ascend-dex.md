**dApp name:** Ascend DEX

**Repository:** [Ascend DEX Repository](https://github.com/Ascend-Perps/ascend-dex)

**Brief description:**
Ascend DEX is a privacy-preserving spot order book where users place limit orders by depositing assets and committing order parameters. Matching occurs off-chain, while on-chain settlement flows verify fills and enforce deposit coverage, limit-price protection, self-trade prevention, recipient-owner binding, fee validation, and color separation between assets.

| Category            | Score | Summary                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| ------------------- | ----- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Privacy-at-risk     | 2     | Ascend DEX keeps order parameters (side, price, amount, owner, secret) private through transient-hash commitments and ZK proofs, with only deposits and settlement outputs (`fillAmount`, `fillPrice`, recipient, fee) exposed on-chain. The off-chain matcher is within the trust boundary since it accesses witness data for matching, but counterparties cannot infer hidden order details. |
| Value-at-risk       | 2     | Funds are escrowed per-order until settlement or cancellation, without pooled liquidity exposure. Security relies on nullifiers, Merkle inclusion checks, deposit coverage validation, immutable fee configuration, fee bounds, limit-price enforcement, recipient-owner binding, and self-trade prevention. Blast radius is limited to currently open-order capital.                          |
| State-space-at-risk | 1     | State growth is strictly bounded by a depth-14 Merkle tree with a maximum of 16,384 commitments. Duplicate commitments are rejected, placements halt at capacity, and permanent nullifiers prevent replay or double-spend attacks even after history resets.                                                                                                                                   |
